Our History

Qanaqa-e-Sabriya was established in 1997 by the descendants of Hazrat Ibrahim Balkhi (R.H) of Old Kodangal, tracing their lineage to the blessed family of Abdul Qadir Jilani (R.A).

We serve the community through monthly langars, zikr gatherings, qawwali mehfils, and spiritual development sessions. The Sajjada Nasheen continues the path of Sufi love and service to mankind.
